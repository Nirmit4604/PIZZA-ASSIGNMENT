About Us

It's not just about the pizzas. At Slice of Heaven, we take pride in our warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our friendly staff is always ready to assist you in choosing the perfect pizza or customizing one to your liking. Whether you're dining in, taking out, or getting your pizza delivered to your doorstep, we ensure a seamless and delightful experience for all our customers. We value our community and are actively involved in giving back. We support local events, schools, and charities because we believe in sharing the love for pizza and making a positive impact in the lives of those around us.
